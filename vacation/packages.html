Grand Canyon National Park, USA - $500-600 per person

Explore the awe-inspiring Grand Canyon National Park on a budget! Located in northern Arizona, this iconic destination offers breathtaking vistas, endless hiking trails, and stunning sunsets—all without overspending. Enjoy budget-friendly activities like ranger programs, scenic drives, and self-guided hikes. With affordable camping, lodges, and dining options, you can experience the wonder of the canyon without breaking the bank. Start planning your unforgettable and economical adventure today!

Check out the Red Feather Lodge near the South Rim enterance.

If you are flying into the area, rental cars are available at the Flagstaff Pulliam Airport. Reserve a car here!

Paris, France - $1,800 per person

Discover the magic of Paris without overspending! Enjoy free or low-cost activities like strolling along the Seine, marveling at the Eiffel Tower, or exploring vibrant neighborhoods like Montmartre. Take advantage of affordable hostels, budget hotels, and the city’s efficient metro system. Savor classic French cuisine at local cafés or street markets, and visit museums on discounted or free-entry days. With careful planning, Paris can be as enchanting as ever without breaking the bank!

Book a room at the modern Hotel Cabane!

Rental cars starting at $19 a day can be found here.

Banff National Park, Canada - $400 per person

Nestled in the heart of the Canadian Rockies, Banff National Park offers stunning alpine scenery, crystal-clear lakes, and vast outdoor adventures—all without breaking the bank. Whether you’re hiking along the picturesque Lake Louise or exploring the scenic drives through mountain valleys, Banff provides budget-friendly experiences with plenty of free activities like wildlife watching and scenic views. Affordable camping, hostels, and budget hotels in and around the town of Banff offer economical stays. With various low-cost activities like visiting the Banff Upper Hot Springs and easy access to the park’s hiking trails, Banff is the perfect destination for nature lovers on a budget.

Enjoy a room directly by Bow River at the Bow View Lodge.

Rent a car through Budget Rentals starting at $50.
